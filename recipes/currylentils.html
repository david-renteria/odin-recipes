Green Lentil Coconut Curry

Description

This easy to make creamy coconut lentil curry is a healthy vegan recipe that makes a perfect meatless Monday dinner recipe. It takes less than an hour (mostly hands-off time) to make and is packed full of delicious Indian flavors. Make extras and you'll have a giant smile on your face at lunch the next day.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp | cooking oil
  • 1 box | mirepoix
  • 2 tbsp | curry powder
  • 1 cup | green lentils
  • 2.5 cups | vegetable broth
  • 15 oz can | coconut milk
  • 1 tsp | red pepper flakes
  • 1 tsp | salt
  • 1.5 cups | basmati rice
  • 1.875 cups | water
  • 1.5 tbsp | unsalted butter
  • garlic naan (optional)
  • Greek yogurt (optional)

Steps

  1. Rince rice thoroughly 203 times, or until water runs almost clear.
  2. Add water and butter to cooking device (e.g. rice cooker).
  3. Set aside, but don't start cooking the rice yet.
  4. Saute mirepoix mix for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally
  5. Add curry powder and cook for a few more minutes, stirring occasionally
  6. Add lentils and vegetable broth, bring to a simmer, reduce heat to low, cover with a lid, and cook for 20 minutes.
  7. Start cooking the rice.
  8. Once the lentils are done cooking, remove lid and add the coconut milk, red pepper flakes, and salt. Then cook for an additional 10 minutes on low-medium heat.
  9. Pre-heat oven to 400F.
  10. The lentils and rice should finish cooking around the same time. Once finished, stir/fluff, then set aside.
  11. Once the oven is done pre-heating, add a garlic naan slice and bake for 3-3.5 minutes (or longer if you prefer crunchier naan).
  12. After the garlic naan is finished, everything is ready to be served!
  13. (optional) Add a scoop of Greek yogurt on top of the lentils for extra creaminess.
