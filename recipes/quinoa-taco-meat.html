Quinoa Taco Meat

Description

This is the perfect filling for all your Mexican night needs: Tacos, burrito bowls, taco salads, nachos, enchiladas, tostadas (also in the cookbook) - you name it, it works!

Ingredients

  • 0.5 cup | uncooked quinoa (white, red, or tri-color is fine)
  • 0.5 cup | vegetable broth
  • 3 oz | water
  • 0.25 cup | salsa (mild, medium, or hot)
  • 0.5 tsp | nutritional yeast
  • 1 tsp | cumin
  • 1 tsp | chili powder
  • 0.25 tsp | garlic powder
  • 0.25 tsp | salt
  • 0..25 tsp | black pepper
  • 0.5 tbsp | olive oil

Steps

  1. Rinse quinoa and toast in pan for 4-5 minutes over medium heat. Stir frequently.
  2. Add vegetable broth and water and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low, cover with lid, and cook for 15-20 minutes (or until liquid is fully absorbed). Fluff the quinoa, crack the lid, and let rest for 10 minutes off heat.
  3. Preheat oven to 375F.
  4. Add cooked quinoa to a large mixing bown and mix in remaining ingredients.
  5. Spread mix onto a lightly greased or parchment-lined baking sheet.
  6. Bake in the oven for 10 minutes, stir/toss, then bake for an additional 10-15 minutes.
  7. The quinoa is done once fragrant and golden brown. Be careful not to overcook!
